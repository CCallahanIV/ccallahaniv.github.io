self-improvement is an iterative process

This website is my attempt to prove to myself that a lapsed Mechanical Engineer can find new purpose through passion for technology, thirst for new challenges, and severe allergic reactions to complacency.

I believe that life's many challenges are opportunities to improve yourself. The experience and knowledge gained from each new test is a means to iteratively create a better self. My hope is that by constantly challenging myself I can continually improve.
